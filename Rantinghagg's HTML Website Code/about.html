About The Hagg

Who am I? I'm just a person with a keyboard and a very low tolerance for nonsense. RantingHagg was born in 2026 as a digital catharsis for the absolute absurdity of modern life.

I don't have "actionable insights." I don't have "core competencies." I have opinions that nobody asked for, and I'm sharing them anyway because... well, because I can. If you're looking for positivity, go find a "live, laugh, love" sign at Home Depot. This is not the place for you.

If you're looking for someone to say the things you're thinking during your Tuesday morning "sync," then welcome. Grab a coffee, sit down, and prepare to be mildly annoyed alongside me.

The Hagg is not a brand. It's a mood. A dark, cynical, highly caffeinated mood.
