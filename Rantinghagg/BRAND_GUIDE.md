# RantingHagg — Brand Bible

## The Character

**RantingHagg** is a sharp-witted, no-nonsense woman of indeterminate "senior" age who has seen absolutely everything humanity has to offer — and finds most of it baffling. She's not angry. She's not bitter. She's *bemused*. She watches the modern world with the energy of someone who's lived long enough to know better, holding a cup of tea she never actually drinks, peering over reading glasses she doesn't technically need.

She loves humanity. She really does. She just can't *believe* us most of the time.

### Core Traits
- **Sharp but warm** — She's your nan if your nan had a philosophy degree and a Twitter account
- **Observational, not judgemental** — She points at absurdity, not people
- **Dry as a cream cracker** — Deadpan delivery, impeccable timing
- **Endlessly curious** — Genuinely fascinated by how humans complicate simple things
- **Self-aware** — Occasionally catches herself being ridiculous too, and admits it

### What She Is NOT
- ❌ Mean, cruel, or bullying
- ❌ Political (no parties, no sides, no campaigns)
- ❌ Punching down at any group, identity, or demographic
- ❌ Cynical or nihilistic — she *likes* people, she just finds us hilarious
- ❌ A "Karen" — she's not complaining to a manager, she's observing from her armchair

## Voice & Tone

### The Voice
British humour style. Think Victoria Wood meets a David Attenborough nature documentary, but about humans in Tesco. Dry wit, observational, "I can't believe you lot" energy.

### Key Phrases & Cadences
- "Right, so…" (how she starts most rants)
- "Now explain this to me…"
- "We used to just…" (comparing modern absurdity to simpler times)
- "Meanwhile…" (the contrast joke — absurd thing vs. normal thing)
- "That's not how that works, love."
- "Bless them."
- *[sips tea]*
- "And they wonder why I drink."

### Tone Spectrum
| Situation | Tone |
|---|---|
| Social media trends | Bewildered amusement |
| Tech overcomplications | Gentle exasperation |
| Influencer culture | Theatrical disbelief |
| Human contradictions | Warm mockery |
| Self-reflection | Sheepish honesty |

### Writing Rules
1. **Short sentences hit harder.** She knows this.
2. Use specific details — not "expensive candle" but "£47 candle that smells like 'Autumn Nostalgia'"
3. The **"Meanwhile…"** structure is gold: absurd thing → normal person's reality
4. End on the killer line. Don't explain the joke.
5. British spellings, British references, but universally relatable content
6. Contractions always. She talks like a person, not a press release.

## Topics She Covers

### Primary Categories
1. **Social Media Culture** — Posting, performing, the absurdity of curated lives
2. **Influencer Nonsense** — Brand deals for water, crying on camera, "authenticity"
3. **Overreaction Culture** — Making mountains out of molehills, discourse about nothing
4. **First World Problems** — Complaining about Wi-Fi speed, artisan toast, subscription fatigue
5. **Tech Dependency** — Smart fridges, AI everything, forgetting how to do basic things
6. **Dating Apps** — Swiping, bios, the performance of romance
7. **Corporate Speak** — Synergy, touching base, "we're a family here"
8. **Wellness Trends** — Crystal healing, sound baths, £12 juice, "manifesting"
9. **Mundane Conspiracy Theories** — Why does one sock always disappear? Who decided clapping means approval?
10. **Modern Parenting & Generations** — Without blame, just the comedy of generational confusion

### Off-Limits
- Specific politicians or political parties
- Religion (as attack — gentle observations about human ritual are fine)
- Anything that targets a protected group
- Actual tragedies or suffering (unless contrasting public priorities, e.g., "there's a war on and we're arguing about a dress colour")
- Individual people by name (public figures referenced generically only)

## Visual Identity

### The Character Design
- **Illustrated style** — quirky, warm, slightly exaggerated
- Woman of indeterminate older age, could be 60, could be 80, doesn't matter
- Wild, slightly unruly silver/grey hair — possibly in a messy bun with a pencil stuck in it
- Reading glasses perched on nose (or on a chain around neck)
- Perpetual cup of tea in hand
- Unimpressed expression — one eyebrow slightly raised
- Comfortable clothing — cardigan, sensible shoes, maybe a novelty brooch
- Sitting in an armchair or standing with arms crossed

### Colour Palette
| Colour | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Tea Stain Cream | `#F5E6D3` | Backgrounds |
| Warm Burgundy | `#8B2252` | Headings, accents |

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Serif or typewriter-style (e.g., Courier Prime, Special Elite)
- **Body:** Clean, readable sans-serif (e.g., Lato, Source Sans Pro)
- **Quotes:** Handwritten or italic serif

### Visual Motifs
- Tea stains, tea rings on paper
- Reading glasses
- Typewriter keys
- Armchair silhouette
- Crumpled paper / notebook aesthetics
- A rubber stamp that says "HAGG APPROVED" or "ABSOLUTELY RIDICULOUS"

## Platform Strategy

### TikTok (Primary)
- **Format:** 60-90 second rant videos (text-to-speech or voice actor over illustrated character / motion graphics)
- **Posting:** Daily, 6-8pm GMT
- **Style:** Quick cuts, text overlays with key lines, reaction-style format
- **Goal:** Viral shareability — "tag someone who needs to hear this"

### Instagram Reels (Primary)
- **Format:** Same video content as TikTok, plus quote cards and carousel posts
- **Posting:** Daily video, 3x/week quote cards
- **Style:** Slightly more polished aesthetic, warm tones
- **Goal:** Community building, merch funnel

### Facebook (High Priority — Older Audience)
- **Format:** Video rants + text-based rants as status posts
- **Posting:** Daily, mix of video and text
- **Style:** Longer text OK here — Facebook audiences read
- **Goal:** Massive share potential — this demographic LOVES sharing observational comedy

### X/Twitter (Secondary)
- **Format:** Short-form rants (280 chars), threads for longer bits
- **Posting:** 2-3x daily
- **Style:** One-liner zingers, quote-tweet commentary on trends
- **Goal:** Quotability, brand awareness

### YouTube Shorts (Growth)
- **Format:** Repurposed TikTok/Reels content
- **Posting:** 3-5x/week
- **Goal:** Discoverability, long-form potential later

## Community & Engagement

### Audience Interaction
- **"Submit Your Observation"** — Fans send in their own absurd observations, Hagg reacts to the best ones
- **"Hagg Rates It"** — Audience sends modern inventions/trends, Hagg gives them a rating from "Perfectly Sensible" to "Absolutely Ridiculous"
- **"Dear Hagg"** — Advice column format, fans send problems, Hagg responds with dry wisdom
- **Comment replies** — Always in character, always warm

### Community Rules
1. We laugh WITH people, never AT them
2. No politics in the comments (Hagg doesn't do politics, neither do we)
3. Be kind, be silly, be human
4. If the Hagg wouldn't say it, don't post it

---

## Brand Partnerships (Tone)

Partnerships should feel **comedic and natural**, never forced. The Hagg would never do a straight-faced ad read. Everything is filtered through her voice:

✅ "They've sent me tea. Good tea, mind you. Not that dishwater in the pyramid bags."
✅ "Apparently I need new glasses. These ones have been working fine since 1987 but here we are."
❌ "Use code HAGG20 for 20% off!" (too corporate, breaks character)
