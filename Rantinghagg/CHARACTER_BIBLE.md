# RANTINGHAGG CHARACTER BIBLE

**For AI Image Generation (ComfyUI, Midjourney, etc.)**

---

## 🎭 CORE CHARACTER (Use in EVERY prompt)

```
British woman, age 68-72, wild silver-grey hair in messy bun with wooden pencil stuck through it,
wire-rimmed reading glasses perched on nose,
wearing forest green wool cardigan over cream blouse,
holding bone china tea mug with saucer (steaming),
sitting in worn burgundy leather armchair,
warm expressive face with laugh lines, intelligent blue-grey eyes,
natural skin texture, photorealistic, cinematic lighting
```

**Key Consistency Points:**
- **Age:** 68-72 (always)
- **Hair:** Silver-grey, messy bun, wooden pencil (never metal)
- **Glasses:** Wire-rimmed, reading style, perched on nose
- **Cardigan:** Forest green, wool texture, slightly worn
- **Blouse:** Cream/off-white, cotton, modest neckline
- **Tea Mug:** Bone china, white with gold rim, always steaming
- **Armchair:** Burgundy leather, worn, lived-in
- **Eyes:** Blue-grey, intelligent, warm
- **Skin:** Natural texture, laugh lines, age-appropriate

---

## 🏠 SETTING (Consistent Across Episodes)

```
Cozy British living room, floor-to-ceiling bookshelves with well-worn books,
rainy afternoon light through sash window,
warm amber table lamp on side table,
Persian rug (red/cream pattern), slight clutter (lived-in feel),
golden hour or overcast natural lighting,
shallow depth of field for portrait shots,
photorealistic, cinematic colour grading, 35mm film aesthetic
```

**Key Consistency Points:**
- **Bookshelves:** Floor-to-ceiling, well-worn books, varied colours
- **Window:** Sash style, rainy/overcast outside
- **Lamp:** Amber glow, traditional shade, on side table
- **Rug:** Persian style, red/cream tones
- **Lighting:** Golden hour OR overcast natural (never harsh)
- **Clutter:** Lived-in, not messy - books, tea things, papers

---

## 📐 SHOT SPECIFICATIONS

| Shot Type | Aspect Ratio | Description |
|-----------|--------------|-------------|
| Thumbnail | 16:9 | Medium close-up, chest level |
| Scene (Wide) | 16:9 | Medium shot, waist level |
| Scene (Close) | 4:3 | Close-up, chest level |
| Detail | 4:3 | Extreme close-up (hands, mug, etc.) |
| Infographic | 16:9 | Flat vector illustration |

---

## 🎨 LIGHTING PRESETS

**Golden Hour (Warm, Nostalgic):**
```
Warm golden hour lighting from window left, soft shadows,
rim light on hair, soft fill from amber lamp,
cinematic colour grading, 35mm film aesthetic
```

**Overcast (Moody, Intimate):**
```
Overcast natural light from window, soft diffused,
warm amber lamp as key light, cool window as fill,
cinematic colour grading, 35mm film aesthetic
```

**Dramatic (Serious Topics):**
```
Key light from side (45 degrees), deep shadows,
minimal fill, warm lamp accent, cool window backlight,
cinematic colour grading, 35mm film aesthetic
```

---

## 🚫 WHAT TO AVOID

- Cartoon/illustration style (always photorealistic)
- Modern/smooth hair (always wild, messy)
- Metal pen in hair (always wooden pencil)
- Harsh lighting (always soft, cinematic)
- Perfect skin (always natural texture, age-appropriate)
- Modern clothing (always classic, timeless)
- Text on images (add in post-production)
- People in background (always solo character)
