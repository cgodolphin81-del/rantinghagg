## 📊 Key Stats

- **Gig Workers:** 15 million gig workers in UK (2025)
- **Gig Platforms:** Uber, Deliveroo, TaskRabbit, Fiverr
- **Income:** Average £18k/year (below living wage)
- **Benefits:** Zero employment rights, no sick pay, no holiday pay
- **Growth:** Gig economy up 300% since 2015

## 💬 Cultural Context

**Gig Economy Promises:**
- "Be your own boss"
- "Flexible hours"
- "Work when you want"
- "Side hustle"

**Gig Economy Reality:**
- "Be your own boss" = you're not
- "Flexible hours" = work when they want
- "Work when you want" = work all the time
- "Side hustle" = main income
- You're not a boss. You're a worker with no rights.
