## Episode Angle

**The Absurdity:** "Be your own boss" means you have no boss, no pay, and no rights. But you do have an app that tells you where to go.

**The Hook:** "You're not self-employed. You're unemployed with an app."

**The Payoff:** "My nan had a job. You have a gig. And the gig is losing."
