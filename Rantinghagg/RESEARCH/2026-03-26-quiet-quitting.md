## 💬 Cultural Context

**What It Is:**
- Working to rule
- No unpaid overtime
- No "going above and beyond"
- Logging off at 5 PM sharp

**What It Isn't:**
- Actually quitting
- Being lazy
- Doing bad work
- Just doing your job properly
