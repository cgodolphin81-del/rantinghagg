**Quiet Living Reality:**
- "Slow down" = hard when everything is fast
- "Live quietly" = hard when you're expected to hustle

- "Rest is productive" = you still feel guilty
- My nan lived quietly. She didn't call it "quiet living."

## 🎯 Rant Angles

1. **"Quiet Living (The Opposite Of Hustle Culture)"**
   - Quiet living is the opposite of hustle culture.
   - "Slow down."
   - "Live quietly."
   - "Find peace."
   - Easy to say. Hard to do.
   - When everything is fast. When you're expected to hustle.
   - My nan lived quietly. She didn't call it "quiet living." She called it "life."

2. **"Rest Is Productive (But I Feel Guilty)"**
   - "Rest is productive."
   - Which means "you should rest."
   - But I feel guilty.
   - 60% of people feel guilty when resting.
   - My nan rested. She didn't call it "productive." She called it "tea time."

3. **"The Quiet Life"**
   - Quiet life = no drama.
   - Quiet life = no hustle.
   - Quiet life = no stress.
   - Quiet life = tea. And biscuits. And rest.
   - My nan had a quiet life. She was happy.
