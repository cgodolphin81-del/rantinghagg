**Digital Nomad Promises:**

- "Be your own boss"
- "Travel the world"
- "Best life"

- "Work from anywhere" = work from cafe
- "Be your own boss" = you're still working

- "Best life" = lonely, burnt out
- You're not living. You're working with wifi.
