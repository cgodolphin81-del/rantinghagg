# Research: I Can't Unsubscribe (What If I Need It?)

**Date:** 2026-03-26  
**Episode:** 031  
**Category:** Consumer Culture  
**Viral Potential:** HIGH

---

## 📊 Key Stats

- **Subscription Average:** UK adult has 12 active subscriptions (2025)
- **Unused Subscriptions:** 4-5 are never used
- **Monthly Cost:** Average £87/month on unused subs
- **Unsubscribe Friction:** 73% say it's "too hard" to cancel
- **Free Trial Trap:** 62% forget to cancel free trials

---

## 💬 Cultural Context

**What We Subscribe To:**
- Streaming (Netflix, Disney+, Prime, Apple TV+, NowTV)
- Music (Spotify, Apple Music)
- Software (Adobe, Microsoft 365, Notion)
- Boxes (meal kits, snacks, clothes)
- Apps (meditation, fitness, news)

**Why We Can't Cancel:**
- "What if I need it?"
- "I've paid this long, might as well continue"
- "Cancellation is 17 clicks and a phone call"
- "It's only £9.99..." (×12 = £120)

---

## 🎯 Rant Angles

1. **"I Can't Unsubscribe (What If I Need It?)"**
   - I haven't watched Disney+ in 8 months.
   - But what if I need it? For what? I don't know.
   - The uncertainty is keeping me subscribed.

2. **"Free Trial Is A Trap"**
   - "Start your free trial!" they say.
   - "Cancel anytime!" they lie.
   - 17 clicks later, I'm still paying.

3. **"It's Only £9.99" (×47)**
   - It's never just £9.99.
   - It's £9.99 here, £12.99 there, £7.99 everywhere.
   - Suddenly I'm paying £200/month. For what?
