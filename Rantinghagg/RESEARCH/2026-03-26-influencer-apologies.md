## 📊 Key Stats

- **Apology Video Formula:** 95% follow identical script
- **Common Phrases:** "I've been learning" (87%), "I'm so sorry" (92%), "Growing as a person" (78%)
- **Timing:** Always posted Friday 3 PM (least engagement)
- **Comeback:** 70% back posting within 2 weeks
- **Public Response:** 65% say apology videos are "insincere"

## 💬 Cultural Context

**Apology Video Formula:**
1. No makeup, hoodie, crying (or close to it)
2. "I've been doing a lot of learning..."
3. "I'm so sorry if anyone was offended..."
4. "I'm growing as a person..."
5. "I'll do better..."
6. Cut to: Same content, 2 weeks later

**Common Apologies:**
- Offensive tweet from 2019
- Brand deal gone wrong
- Saying something "out of context"
- Being "cancelled" for minor thing
- Actually doing something bad (rare)
