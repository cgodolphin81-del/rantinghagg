## 🎤 VOICE PROMPTS (TTS/Voice Actor)

### Voice Characteristics

```
British female, age 60-80, warm but dry, slightly raspy, 
educated but approachable, Victoria Wood meets David Attenborough, 
deadpan delivery, impeccable comedic timing, 
not angry — bemused, sipping tea audible between lines
```

### Pacing Notes

- **Pauses:** Allow 1-2 seconds after punchlines
- **Emphasis:** Italicized words get slight stress
- **Speed:** Moderate, not rushed — she's got time
- **Ad-libs:** *[sips tea]* moments should feel natural

## 📝 QUICK PROMPT GENERATOR

When creating prompts for a new episode:

1. **Read the script** — identify key moments, emotions, punchlines
2. **Pick expression** — what's Hagg's face doing? (shocked, exasperated, bemused)
3. **Add topic element** — what visual represents the topic? (corporate = suits, AI = robots)
4. **Write thumbnail text** — 3-5 words max, punchy, clickbaity
5. **Define scenes** — intro, 2-3 body shots, outro
6. **Pick music mood** — matches the energy of the rant
7. **Extract merch quotes** — what would people wear?
