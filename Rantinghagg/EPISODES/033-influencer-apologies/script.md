# Script: Influencer Apology Videos (I've Been Learning)

**Episode:** 033  
**Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Format:** TikTok/Reels/Shorts  
**Character:** RantingHagg (British woman, 68-72, green cardigan, tea mug)

---

## 🎬 SHOT-BY-SHOT SCRIPT

### Shot 1: Opening - Apology Video (0:00-0:08)

**Visual:** Medium shot, Hagg in armchair, phone in hand, sceptical

**Audio:** "Influencer apology videos all follow the same script. No makeup. Hoodie. Slight sniffle."

**Action:** Mimes no makeup, pulls imaginary hoodie, slight sniffle

---

### Shot 2: Learning (0:08-0:18)

**Visual:** Close-up, mocking

**Audio:** "'I've been doing a lot of learning...' Learning what? How to cry on cue? 'I'm so sorry if anyone was offended...'"

**Action:** Air quotes on "learning", mock crying, then air quotes on "offended"

---

### Shot 3: Blame Shifting (0:18-0:28)

**Visual:** Medium shot, firm

**Audio:** "Not 'I'm sorry I did it.' 'I'm sorry if you were offended.' That's not an apology. That's blame-shifting. With better lighting."

**Action:** Points at camera, firm expression

---

### Shot 4: Growing (0:28-0:38)

**Visual:** Close-up, sceptical

**Audio:** "'I'm growing as a person...' You're growing your follower count. That's all that's growing. 'Taking a break to focus on my mental health.'"

**Action:** Air quotes on "growing", sceptical look

---

### Shot 5: Break Duration (0:38-0:48)

**Visual:** Medium shot, knowing

**Audio:** "Break is 10 days. Then back to daily posts. With a brand deal. Mental health break = content strategy."

**Action:** Holds up 10 fingers, then mimes posting

---

### Shot 6: Nan Comparison (0:48-0:58)

**Visual:** Warm close-up, nostalgic

**Audio:** "My nan apologised by making you a cuppa. 'Sorry love. Tea?' Done. Influencers make a video. 20 minutes long. With chapters."

**Action:** Warm smile, mimes making tea, then mimes video chapters

---

### Shot 7: Outro (0:58-1:05)

**Visual:** Medium shot, slight smirk

**Audio:** "Rant over. Not sorry if you were offended. By this rant."

**Action:** Slight smirk, sip of tea, not sorry

---

- **Pacing:** Moderate, building to firm conclusion
- **Emphasis:** "learning," "if anyone was offended," "10 days," "chapters," "not sorry"
