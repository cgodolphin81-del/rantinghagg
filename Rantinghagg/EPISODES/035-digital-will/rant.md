# What Happens To My Photos When I Die

**Episode:** 035  
**Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Status:** Ready for script

What happens to my 47,000 photos when I die?

Locked. Gone. Forever.

I have 47,000 photos. All on my phone. All digital. All locked behind a password.

My password is "Fluffy123." But I won't tell anyone. Security experts say "use complex password." I use "Fluffy123." And don't tell anyone.

When I die, nobody gets in. Photos lost. Memories gone.

My nan had a box. Under the bed. Everyone looked. Wedding photos. Baby photos. Christmas. All there.

I have a password. I won't share.

40% of photos lost when phone dies. Imagine when I die.

"Cloud is just someone else's computer." With my memories.

When I die, they delete my account. Photos gone. Memories gone.

Digital legacy: 12% have a plan. 88% hope for the best.

I'm in the 88%. Hoping.

My nan's photos survived 50 years. Mine won't survive my password.

"Write it down!" they say. "Share your passwords!"

I'm not writing "Fluffy123" in my will. That's not secure.

So my memories die with me. 47,000 photos. Locked.

Rant over. Backing up my photos. To three places. And telling my mate the password. It's "Fluffy123."
