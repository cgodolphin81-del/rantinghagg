# Script: What Happens To My Photos When I Die

**Episode:** 035  
**Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Format:** TikTok/Reels/Shorts  
**Character:** RantingHagg (British woman, 68-72, green cardigan, tea mug)

---

## 🎬 SHOT-BY-SHOT SCRIPT

### Shot 1: Opening - 47,000 Photos (0:00-0:08)

**Visual:** Medium shot, Hagg in armchair, holding phone, worried

**Audio:** "What happens to my 47,000 photos when I die? Locked. Gone. Forever. I have 47,000 photos. All on my phone. All digital. All locked behind a password."

**Action:** Holds up phone, shows 47,000 (joking), worried expression

---

### Shot 2: Password (0:08-0:18)

**Visual:** Close-up, conspiratorial

**Audio:** "My password is 'Fluffy123.' But I won't tell anyone. Security experts say 'use complex password.' I use 'Fluffy123.' And don't tell anyone."

**Action:** Mimes whispering password, slight laugh

---

### Shot 3: When I Die (0:18-0:28)

**Visual:** Medium shot, sad expression

**Audio:** "When I die, nobody gets in. Photos lost. Memories gone. My nan had a box. Under the bed. Everyone looked. Wedding photos. Baby photos. Christmas. All there."

**Action:** Mimes under bed, nostalgic smile

---

### Shot 4: Cloud (0:28-0:38)

**Visual:** Close-up, firm

**Audio:** "I have a password. I won't share. 40% of photos lost when phone dies. Imagine when I die. 'Cloud is just someone else's computer.' With my memories."

**Action:** Shakes head, air quotes on "cloud"

---

### Shot 5: Digital Legacy (0:38-0:48)

**Visual:** Medium shot, numbers

**Audio:** "When I die, they delete my account. Photos gone. Memories gone. Digital legacy: 12% have a plan. 88% hope for the best. I'm in the 88%. Hoping."

**Action:** Holds up 12 fingers, then 88 fingers (joking), hopeful

---

### Shot 6: Nan vs Password (0:48-0:58)

**Visual:** Close-up, nostalgic then worried

**Audio:** "My nan's photos survived 50 years. Mine won't survive my password. 'Write it down!' they say. 'Share your passwords!' I'm not writing 'Fluffy123' in my will."

**Action:** Warm smile for nan, then serious, shakes head

---

### Shot 7: Outro (0:58-1:05)

**Visual:** Medium shot, determined

**Audio:** "Rant over. Backing up my photos. To three places. And telling my mate the password. It's 'Fluffy123.'"

**Action:** Nods determined, mimes typing, slight laugh

---

## 🎵 AUDIO NOTES

- **Background:** Light acoustic, slightly sad then hopeful tone
- **Pacing:** Moderate, building to determined conclusion
- **Emphasis:** "47,000," "locked," "password," "12%," "Fluffy123"
