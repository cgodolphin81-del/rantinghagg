# Video Script: The Dating Profile Industrial Complex

**Episode:** 008  
**Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Format:** TikTok / Reels / Shorts  
**Voice:** RantingHagg (British, dry, exasperated)

---

## [0:00-0:10] OPENING

*[Hagg in armchair, holding phone, squinting at screen]*

**HAGG:**
Right. Your dating profile.

*[looks at camera]*

Let's talk about it.

You've spent two hours.

Two hours. Of your finite, non-renewable life.

Selecting photos.

---

## [0:10-0:25] THE CURATION

**HAGG:**
Not just picking them.

*[air quotes]*

Selecting.

Like you're curating an exhibition. At the Tate Modern.

*[counts on fingers]*

"This one says I'm adventurous."

"This one says I have a dog."

"This one says I own sunscreen."

*[sips tea]*

---

## [0:25-0:40] THE BIO

**HAGG:**
Then you write your bio.

Which is either...

*[counts on fingers]*

A: A list of demands.

B: Three emojis. And a height.

Or C: "Just ask me a question."

*[deadpan]*

Which is the dating equivalent. Of walking into a job interview.

And saying... "Just ask me questions."

---

## [0:40-0:55] THE SIX PHOTOS

**HAGG:**
And everyone's got the same six photos.

The hiking one.

The one with the fish.

The one where they're "accidentally" shirtless.

*[counts faster]*

The one with the sunglasses on.

The one at a wedding — whose wedding? Who knows.

And the one with the dog. That isn't theirs.

---

## [0:55-1:10] THE KILLER LINE

**HAGG:**
You've got a better marketing team. Than most small businesses.

*[leans forward]*

A small business owner is sitting there thinking... "Should I get a website?"

And you're over here. A/B testing your profile picture.

For maximum swipe velocity.

---

## [1:10-1:15] OUTRO

**HAGG:**
Here's a thought.

Just say you like eating.

And being occasionally nice to people.

*[sips tea]*

See what happens.

Rant over.

*[fade out]*
