# The Dating Profile Industrial Complex

**Episode:** 008  
**Category:** Modern Romance  
**Source:** Content Calendar + Research  
**Status:** Ready for script

---

Right. Your dating profile. Let's talk about it.

You've spent two hours. Two hours of your finite, non-renewable life. Selecting photos. Not just picking them. Selecting. Like you're curating an exhibition at the Tate Modern.

"This one says I'm adventurous." "This one says I have a dog." "This one says I own sunscreen."

Then you write your bio. Which is either: a) a list of demands, b) three emojis and a height, or c) "Just ask me a question." Which is the dating equivalent of walking into a job interview and saying "Just ask me questions."

And everyone's got the same six photos. The hiking one. The one with the fish. The one where they're "accidentally" shirtless. The one with the sunglasses on. The one at a wedding (whose wedding? Who knows). And the one with the dog that isn't theirs.

You've got a better marketing team than most small businesses. A small business owner is sitting there thinking "Should I get a website?" and you're over here A/B testing your profile picture for maximum swipe velocity.

Here's a thought. Just say you like eating and being occasionally nice to people. See what happens.
