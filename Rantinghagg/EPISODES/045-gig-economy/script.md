# Script: Gig Economy (Be Your Own Boss. Unemployed.)

**Episode:** 045  
**Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Format:** TikTok/Reels/Shorts

## 🎬 SHOT-BY-SHOT SCRIPT

### Shot 1: Opening - Be Your Own Boss (0:00-0:08)

**Visual:** Medium shot, Hagg in armchair, sceptical

**Audio:** "'Be your own boss.' I'm not a boss. I'm a worker."

**Action:** Air quotes, sceptical head shake

---

### Shot 2: The Work (0:08-0:18)

**Visual:** Close-up, listing

**Audio:** "I deliver food. I drive people. I do tasks. I have no rights. No sick pay. No holiday pay."

**Action:** Counting on fingers, each task

---

### Shot 3: The Translation (0:18-0:28)

**Visual:** Medium shot, firm

**Audio:** "'Be your own boss' = you're unemployed. But you work. Gig economy: 15 million workers. £18k/year. Below living wage."

**Action:** Firm expression, holds up 15 fingers (joking)

---

### Shot 4: Flexible Hours (0:28-0:38)

**Visual:** Close-up, air quotes

**Audio:** "'Flexible hours' = work when they want. 'Work when you want' = work when the app says."

**Action:** Air quotes, frustrated

---

### Shot 5: The Algorithm (0:38-0:48)

**Visual:** Medium shot, firm

**Audio:** "Your boss is an algorithm. It decides your pay. Your hours. Your work. You can't argue."

**Action:** Points at phone, firm expression

---

### Shot 6: Nan Comparison (0:48-0:58)

**Visual:** Warm close-up, nostalgic

**Audio:** "My nan's boss was a person. I can talk to them. My boss is code. It doesn't talk."

**Action:** Warm smile, then resigned

---

### Shot 7: Outro (0:58-1:05)

**Visual:** Medium shot, accepting gig

**Audio:** "Rant over. Accepting the next gig. Because rent is due."

**Action:** Accepts gig on phone, resigned

---
