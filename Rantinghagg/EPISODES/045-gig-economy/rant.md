"Be your own boss."

I'm not a boss. I'm a worker.

I deliver food. I drive people. I do tasks.

I have no rights. No sick pay. No holiday pay.

"Be your own boss" = you're unemployed. But you work.

Gig economy: 15 million workers. £18k/year. Below living wage.

"Flexible hours" = work when they want.

"Work when you want" = work when the app says.

"Side hustle" = main income.

Your boss is an algorithm.

It decides your pay. Your hours. Your work.

You can't argue. You can't complain.

You can only accept. Or be deactivated.

My nan's boss was a person. I can talk to them.

My boss is code. It doesn't talk.

Gig economy up 300% since 2015.

You're not a boss. You're a worker with no rights.

Rant over. Accepting the next gig. Because rent is due.
