# Video Script: I Feel Guilty For Breathing

**Episode:** 016  
**Duration:** 75-90 seconds  
**Format:** TikTok / Reels / Shorts  
**Voice:** RantingHagg (British, dry, resigned)

---

## [0:00-0:10] OPENING

*[Hagg in armchair, looking tired, holding tea]*

**HAGG:**
I feel guilty. For breathing.

*[pauses]*

Apparently my carbon footprint. Is too big.

And I'm supposed to save the planet.

---

## [0:10-0:25] THE STRAWS

**HAGG:**
By buying eight-pound reusable straws.

While one billionaire's yacht. Emits more. In a day.

Than I will. In a lifetime.

*[sips tea]*

But sure. It's about my plastic bag.

Not the oil companies.

---

## [0:25-0:40] THE LIST

**HAGG:**
I can't have kids. Because of the climate.

I can't fly. Because of the climate.

I can't eat meat. Because of the climate.

I can't buy clothes. Because of fast fashion.

I can't buy new clothes. Because of manufacturing emissions.

*[pauses]*

I can't exist. Basically.

---

## [0:40-0:55] THE RECYCLING

**HAGG:**
And the recycling. Oh, the recycling.

I wash out my yogurt pots.

I separate my glass. From my plastic. From my paper.

I feel like a good person.

*[shakes head]*

Then I find out. Most of it goes to landfill anyway.

Because China stopped taking our recycling.

Because it's not economically viable.

Because the system is broken.

---

## [0:55-1:10] NAN COMPARISON

**HAGG:**
My nan didn't have climate anxiety.

She had a war. And rationing.

And she made do. And she mended.

And she didn't have a carbon footprint.

Because she didn't have a footprint.

She had a pair of shoes.

And she wore them. Until they fell apart.

---

## [1:10-1:20] THE TRUTH

**HAGG:**
We're not saving the planet.

We're performing virtue.

While the planet burns.

---

## [1:20-1:25] OUTRO

**HAGG:**
And I feel guilty.

For breathing.

Rant over.

*[fade out on her looking at tea]*
