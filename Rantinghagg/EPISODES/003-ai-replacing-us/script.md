# Video Script: Will AI Replace Me? Hopefully.

**Episode:** 003  
**Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Format:** TikTok / Reels / Shorts  
**Voice:** RantingHagg (British, dry, bemused)

---

## [0:00-0:05] OPENING

*[Hagg sitting in armchair, adjusts glasses, picks up tea]*

**HAGG:**
I read an article today. "Sixty percent of white-collar jobs will be automated by 2030."

*[sips tea]*

Most people are terrified. Updating their LinkedIn. Learning to code. Crying into their lattes.

---

## [0:05-0:15] THE HOOK

**HAGG:**
Not me. I'm cheering on the robots from the sidelines. With a giant foam finger.

*[gestures with foam finger or exaggerated arm movement]*

Please. Let AI take my job. I'm *begging* you.

---

## [0:15-0:30] THE RANT

**HAGG:**
If a silicon chip wants to spend eight hours moving numbers from one spreadsheet to another... be my guest.

If an LLM wants to write "friendly reminder" emails that aren't actually friendly... have at it.

*[sips tea]*

I'll even provide the electricity.

---

## [0:30-0:50] THE PAYOFF

**HAGG:**
What are we so afraid of losing? 

The privilege of sitting in a grey cubicle... under fluorescent lights that buzz like angry bees?

The joy of "re-aligning" with Bill from Accounting... who smells like mothballs and disappointment?

*[shakes head, sips tea]*

---

## [0:50-1:05] THE TWIST

**HAGG:**
If AI takes over, maybe we can finally go back to what humans were meant to do.

Nap. Tell stories. Occasionally forage for berries.

*[leans forward, direct to camera]*

The AI isn't replacing "the human spirit." It's replacing the part of us that pretends to care about "projected synergies for Q4."

---

## [1:05-1:15] OUTRO

**HAGG:**
And I say... good riddance.

Let the machines handle the spreadsheets. I'll handle the napping.

*[picks up tea, waves dismissively]*

Come at me, GPT-5.

*[sips tea. Fade out.]*
