# Will AI Replace Me? Hopefully.

**Episode:** 003  
**Category:** AI Apocalypse  
**Published:** March 22, 2026  
**URL:** https://rantinghagg.netlify.app/rants/ai-replacing-us.html

---

I read an article today about "The AI Takeover" and how "60% of all white-collar jobs will be automated by 2030." Most people are terrified. They're updating their LinkedIn profiles, learning how to code, and crying into their lattes. Not me. I'm cheering on the robots from the sidelines with a giant foam finger.

Please, let AI take my job. I'm begging you. If a silicon chip wants to spend eight hours a day moving numbers from one spreadsheet to another, be my guest. If an LLM wants to write "friendly reminder" emails that aren't actually friendly, have at it. I'll even provide the electricity.

> **"I'M ACTUALLY ROOTING FOR THE MACHINES TO TAKE OVER MY 9-TO-5."**

What are we so afraid of losing? The privilege of sitting in a grey cubicle under fluorescent lights that buzz like a swarm of angry bees? The joy of "re-aligning" with Bill from Accounting who smells like mothballs and disappointment? If AI takes over, maybe we can finally go back to what humans were meant to do: nap, tell stories, and occasionally forage for berries.

The AI isn't going to replace "the human spirit." It's going to replace the part of us that has to pretend to care about "projected synergies for Q4." And I say: good riddance. Let the machines handle the spreadsheets while I handle the napping. I'm ready to be automated out of existence. Come at me, GPT-5.

Rant over. I'm going to go stare at a wall until the robots arrive.
