# Video Script: I Don't Own Anything Anymore

**Episode:** 010  
**Duration:** 75-90 seconds  
**Format:** TikTok / Reels / Shorts  
**Voice:** RantingHagg (British, dry, slightly angry)

---

## [0:00-0:10] OPENING

*[Hagg in armchair, holding an old kettle]*

**HAGG:**
My nan. Bought a kettle.

*[holds it up]*

It boiled water.

She owned it. Until she died.

*[pauses]*

Revolutionary.

---

## [0:10-0:25] THE LIST

**HAGG:**
Now?

Now your kettle is a subscription.

Your car is a subscription.

Your music. Is a subscription.

Your movies. Your appliances. Your heating.

*[counts on fingers, getting faster]*

Even the heated seats. In your car.

You can't have them. Unless you pay fifteen pounds a month.

To keep them heated.

---

## [0:25-0:40] THE REALITY

**HAGG:**
I'm not joking.

You buy a fifty-thousand-pound car.

And the heated seats. Are "as a service."

*[air quotes]*

You have to subscribe. To warmth.

*[sips tea, genuinely baffled]*

---

## [0:40-0:55] THE PATTERN

**HAGG:**
We're renting our lives. And calling it convenience.

*"Oh, you don't want to buy the software? Just subscribe!"*

*"Oh, you don't want to buy the movie? Just subscribe!"*

*"Oh, you don't want to own your furniture? Just subscribe!"*

*[sets mug down firmly]*

---

## [0:55-1:10] THE WORST PART

**HAGG:**
And the worst part?

You forget to cancel.

You're trying to cancel your gym membership. Your streaming service. Your phone plan. Your heating subscription. Your car subscription.

*[leans forward]*

And somewhere. A company. Is charging you three hundred pounds.

For something you don't even use.

*[pauses]*

"Forgot to cancel."

Is now a lifestyle.

---

## [1:10-1:25] THE COMPARISON

**HAGG:**
And we're okay with this.

We're okay with not owning anything.

We're okay with our lives. Being a series of monthly payments.

That can be cancelled. At any time.

*[points at camera]*

By them.

Your nan bought a kettle.

She owned it.

It boiled water.

It was hers.

She didn't have to pay five pounds a month. To feel the warmth.

---

## [1:25-1:30] OUTRO

**HAGG:**
We're not living in convenience anymore.

*[sets kettle down]*

We're living in rent.

Rant over.

*[fade out on her shaking head]*
