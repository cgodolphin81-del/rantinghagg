# Video Script: Everyone's An Influencer Now

**Episode:** 011  
**Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Format:** TikTok / Reels / Shorts  
**Voice:** RantingHagg (British, dry, amused)

---

## [0:00-0:10] OPENING

*[Hagg in armchair, scrolling phone, looks up]*

**HAGG:**
Right. Everyone's an influencer now.

*[pauses]*

Even people who sell things. Nobody bought.

Your mate who sells candles. Is now a "content creator."

---

## [0:10-0:20] THE HOBBY

**HAGG:**
Your nan knew what that was.

*[sips tea]*

It was called... having a hobby.

She knitted. She watched Corrie. She didn't have brand deals. For her knitting.

---

## [0:20-0:35] THE BRAND DEALS

**HAGG:**
And the brand deals. Oh, the brand deals.

Someone's sent you free water. And now you have to post about it.

*"Hydration is self-care, babes!"*

*[deadpan]*

It's water. From a tap. Or a bottle. That cost eighty pence.

---

## [0:35-0:50] THE DAY IN MY LIFE

**HAGG:**
Then there's the "day in my life" videos.

Which is just you. In your house. Doing nothing.

But with better lighting. And a ring light. And fourteen camera angles.

*[mimes filming]*

Of you. Making toast.

---

## [0:50-1:05] THE VULNERABILITY

**HAGG:**
"Get ready with me: therapy edition."

You're not going to therapy. You're going to Pret.

But you need to film it. For the story.

And the crying. Someone's always crying on camera now.

*"Being vulnerable with you guys."*

*[shakes head]*

You're not being vulnerable. You're getting views.

There's a difference.

---

## [1:05-1:15] OUTRO

**HAGG:**
My nan had hobbies.

She didn't have brand deals.

She just... did things. For fun.

Without an audience.

*[sips tea]*

Revolutionary.

Rant over.

*[fade out]*
