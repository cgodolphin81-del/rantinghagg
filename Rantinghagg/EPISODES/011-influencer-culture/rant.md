# Everyone's An Influencer Now

**Episode:** 011  
**Category:** Influencer Culture  
**Source:** Content Calendar + Research  
**Status:** Ready for script

---

Right. Everyone's an influencer now. Even people who sell things nobody bought.

Your mate who sells candles is now a "content creator." Your nan knew what that was. It was called "having a hobby."

And the brand deals. Oh, the brand deals. Someone's sent you free water. And now you have to post about it. "Hydration is self-care, babes!" It's water. From a tap. Or a bottle. That cost 80p.

Then there's the "day in my life" videos. Which is just you. In your house. Doing nothing. But with better lighting. And a ring light. And fourteen camera angles. Of you. Making toast.

"Get ready with me: therapy edition." You're not going to therapy. You're going to Pret. But you need to film it. For the story.

And the crying. Someone's always crying on camera now. "Being vulnerable with you guys." You're not being vulnerable. You're getting views. There's a difference.

My nan had hobbies. She knitted. She watched Corrie. She didn't have brand deals for her knitting. She didn't film herself watching Corrie and call it "content."

She just... did things. For fun. Without an audience.

Revolutionary.
