# Video Script: Stop Buying These 5-Step Success Books

**Episode:** 002  
**Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Format:** TikTok / Reels / Shorts  
**Voice:** RantingHagg (British, dry, thoroughly unimpressed)

---

## [0:00-0:08] OPENING

*[Hagg holding a self-help book, looks at it with suspicion]*

**HAGG:**
I went to a bookstore today. It was a mistake.

*[tosses book aside]*

Ended up in the "Self-Help" section. Which is basically a five-thousand-square-foot monument to human insecurity.

---

## [0:08-0:20] THE HOOK

**HAGG:**
Every book has a title like... "The Zen of Zero Effs."

*[picks up another imaginary book, reads]*

Or... "How to Manifest Your First Billion by Thinking Really Hard."

*[looks at camera, deadpan]*

I'm sick of it.

---

## [0:20-0:35] THE RANT

**HAGG:**
These books are all the same. Written by a guy named "Chad."

*[air quotes]*

Retired at twenty-two. After "grinding" for three weeks.

*[sips tea]*

If you look closely, Step One is always... "My dad gave me a small loan of five million dollars. And a Porsche."

---

## [0:35-0:50] THE MORNING ROUTINE

**HAGG:**
Then there's the morning routine obsession.

*[counts on fingers]*

Wake up at 4 AM. Drink a smoothie made of kale and sadness. Meditate for two hours.

Write a list of one hundred things you're grateful for... before you even look at a screen.

*[pauses]*

By 8 AM, you're so exhausted from "becoming your best self"...

*[mimes falling asleep]*

...you have to nap for the rest of the day.

---

## [0:50-1:05] THE KILLER LINE

**HAGG:**
It's professional gaslighting.

*[leans forward, direct to camera]*

If you're not successful, it's because you didn't "want it enough." You didn't "vibrate at the right frequency."

*[scoffs]*

No. Maybe your rent is ninety percent of your income. But sure. Let's "re-wire your brain."

---

## [1:05-1:15] OUTRO

**HAGG:**
Here's my one-step success guide.

*[holds up one finger]*

Stop buying books... written by people who make money... selling books about how to make money.

*[sips tea]*

It's an infinite loop of nonsense.

Go be mediocre. It's fine.

*[waves. Fade out.]*
