# Video Script: Your Screen Time Report Came In

**Episode:** 012  
**Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Format:** TikTok / Reels / Shorts  
**Voice:** RantingHagg (British, dry, concerned)

---

## [0:00-0:10] OPENING

*[Hagg in armchair, holding phone, looking at screen]*

**HAGG:**
Your screen time report came in.

*[looks at camera]*

Eight hours.

You have a part-time job. With your phone.

And you don't even get paid.

---

## [0:10-0:25] THE COMPARISON

**HAGG:**
You spend more time with your phone. Than your partner.

And your phone doesn't even complain.

It doesn't say "you're always on that thing."

It just... waits. Patiently. For your next scroll.

---

## [0:25-0:40] THE HABITS

**HAGG:**
You can't eat without it.

Can't go to the toilet without it.

Can't walk down the street without checking it.

*"Just quickly."*

*[shakes head]*

Just quickly turns into two hours.

And now you're late. And hungry. And still scrolling.

---

## [0:40-0:55] THE ANXIETY

**HAGG:**
Your battery anxiety kicks in. At eighty percent.

Eighty.

You're sweating. Looking for a charger. Like you're dying.

*[sips tea]*

My nan picked up a newspaper. Once a day.

And she was done.

---

## [0:55-1:10] THE REALITY

**HAGG:**
She read it. She put it down. She went about her day.

You pick up a portal to hell.

And you live there.

And the phantom vibrations.

You feel your phone buzz.

It didn't.

Your brain is now inventing notifications.

---

## [1:10-1:15] OUTRO

**HAGG:**
You're being haunted.

By a phone. That isn't even ringing.

Your screen time: eight hours.

Your life: passing you by.

Rant over.

*[fade out]*
