# Your Screen Time Report Came In

**Episode:** 012  
**Category:** Tech Addiction  
**Source:** Content Calendar + Research  
**Status:** Ready for script

---

Your screen time report came in. Eight hours. You have a part-time job. With your phone.

And you don't even get paid.

You spend more time with your phone than your partner. And your phone doesn't even complain. It doesn't say "you're always on that thing." It just... waits. Patiently. For your next scroll.

You can't eat without it. Can't go to the toilet without it. Can't walk down the street without checking it. "Just quickly." Just quickly turns into two hours. And now you're late. And hungry. And still scrolling.

Your battery anxiety kicks in at eighty percent. Eighty! You're sweating. Looking for a charger. Like you're dying.

My nan picked up a newspaper. Once a day. And she was done. She read it. She put it down. She went about her day.

You pick up a portal to hell. And you live there.

And the phantom vibrations. You feel your phone buzz. It didn't. Your brain is now inventing notifications. You're being haunted. By a phone that isn't even ringing.

Your screen time: eight hours. Your life: passing you by.
