# Video Script: Wellness For People Who Have Money

**Episode:** 009  
**Duration:** 75-90 seconds  
**Format:** TikTok / Reels / Shorts  
**Voice:** RantingHagg (British, dry, warm)

---

## [0:00-0:10] OPENING

*[Hagg in armchair, holding tea, looking content]*

**HAGG:**
My nan's self-care. Was a cuppa. And having a lie-down.

*[sips tea]*

Now?

Now it's £120. To sit in a room. With a singing bowl.

While someone whispers at you.

---

## [0:10-0:25] THE INVESTMENT

**HAGG:**
And don't get me started on the "investment" language.

*"An investment in yourself."*

*[air quotes]*

No, Karen.

It's eighty pounds. For a jar of cream.

That is... moisture.

*[pauses]*

Moisture goes on your face.

That's not an investment.

That's a transaction.

---

## [0:25-0:40] THE CRYSTALS

**HAGG:**
And the crystals.

Oh, the crystals.

*"This one will align your chakras."*

*"This one will manifest abundance."*

*"This one will help you manifest your next paycheck."*

*[sips tea]*

Which is fair.

Because you've spent your entire paycheck. On this crystal.

---

## [0:40-0:55] WHAT ACTUALLY WORKS

**HAGG:**
You know what actually aligns your chakras?

*[leans forward]*

Not having to think about how much you've spent. On self-care.

Not having to worry about your rent.

Not having to pretend. Your living room is a boutique hotel.

---

## [0:55-1:10] THE SOUND BATH

**HAGG:**
And the sound baths.

*"Transformative experience."*

*"Healing frequencies."*

*[shrugs]*

I don't know.

Maybe it's the fact. That I'm not being asked to like and share.

Maybe it's the fact. That I'm not being told. That my aura is wrong.

---

## [1:10-1:20] THE REALITY

**HAGG:**
But no.

We've got "wellness" now.

Which is just capitalism. With better lighting.

*[sets mug down]*

You don't need a crystal.

You need a nap.

You don't need a sound bath.

You need silence.

You don't need a twelve-pound juice.

You need vegetables.

And they're free. If you grow them yourself.

Or a pound fifty. At the supermarket.

---

## [1:20-1:25] OUTRO

**HAGG:**
Rant over.

I'm going to have a lie-down.

*[sips tea]*

It's free.

*[fade out]*
