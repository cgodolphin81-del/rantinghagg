# The Authenticity Video

**Episode:** 005  
**Category:** Social Media Culture  
**Source:** Content Calendar Day 2  
**Status:** Ready for script

---

A man — a grown adult man — spent three hours. Three hours of his finite life on this planet. Editing a fifteen-second video. About authenticity.

Three hours. Choosing the right filter. The right font. The right background music — which was, obviously, that sad piano that plays on every video where someone pretends to be deep. You know the one. Dun… dun dun… dun dun dun.

He did fourteen takes. Fourteen. Of himself saying "just be yourself." He wasn't being himself in any of them. He was being the version of himself that gets the most likes, which is a completely different person.

Then he added captions. Because apparently we can't listen anymore. Then he colour-graded it. A video of his face. He colour-graded his own face. Made himself slightly more orange and called it content.

And at the end — after the lighting adjustment, after the ring light, after the carefully messy hair — he looked into the camera and said, "Stop trying to be perfect."

And three hundred thousand people liked it.

We're cooked, aren't we.
