# Video Script: The Authenticity Video

**Episode:** 005  
**Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Format:** TikTok / Reels / Shorts  
**Voice:** RantingHagg (British, dry, resigned)

---

## [0:00-0:10] OPENING

*[Hagg in armchair, looking at phone with disbelief]*

**HAGG:**
A man — a grown adult man — spent three hours.

*[looks up at camera, lets it land]*

Three hours. Of his finite life on this planet.

Editing a fifteen-second video.

*[pauses]*

About authenticity.

---

## [0:10-0:25] THE BREAKDOWN

**HAGG:**
Three hours. Choosing the right filter. The right font. The right background music.

*[mimes sad piano]*

Dun... dun dun... dun dun dun.

You know the one. That sad piano that plays on every video where someone pretends to be deep.

*[sips tea]*

He did fourteen takes. Fourteen. Of himself saying "just be yourself."

*[deadpan]*

He wasn't being himself in any of them.

---

## [0:25-0:40] THE ABSURDITY

**HAGG:**
He was being the version of himself that gets the most likes.

*[shrugs]*

Which is a completely different person.

Then he added captions. Because apparently we can't listen anymore.

Then he colour-graded it.

*[leans forward]*

A video. Of his face.

He colour-graded his own face.

*[shakes head]*

Made himself slightly more orange. And called it content.

---

## [0:40-0:55] THE KILLER LINE

**HAGG:**
And at the end — after the lighting adjustment, after the ring light, after the carefully messy hair...

*[counts on fingers]*

...he looked into the camera.

And said...

*[mimes deep voice]*

"Stop trying to be perfect."

*[pauses, lets the irony hang]*

---

## [0:55-1:10] THE PAYOFF

**HAGG:**
And three hundred thousand people liked it.

*[long sip of tea]*

*[sets mug down slowly]*

We're cooked, aren't we.

*[not angry. Just... resigned.]*

---

## [1:10-1:15] OUTRO

*[Hagg stares into middle distance for a beat]*

*[Fade out on her existential sigh]*
