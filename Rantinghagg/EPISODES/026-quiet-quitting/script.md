# Script: Quiet Quitting (Doing Exactly What I'm Paid For)

## 🎬 SHOT-BY-SHOT SCRIPT

### Shot 1: Opening - Quiet Quitting (0:00-0:08)

**Visual:** Medium shot, Hagg in armchair, holding tea, looking slightly guilty

**Audio:** "I'm quiet quitting. And I'm rubbish at it. Because quiet quitting is just... working. Normally."

**Action:** Slight shrug, sip of tea

---

### Shot 2: Contract (0:08-0:18)

**Visual:** Close-up, Hagg holds up imaginary contract

**Audio:** "My contract says 9 to 5. I work 9 to 5. Apparently that's radical now. My boss said I need to 'go above and beyond.' I said my contract says 'duties as outlined.' He didn't like that."

**Action:** Air quotes on "above and beyond", slight smirk

---

### Shot 3: Corporate Translation (0:18-0:28)

**Visual:** Medium shot, counting on fingers

**Audio:** "Above and beyond is corporate for 'we're understaffed.' And you're not paying me for it. 'Go the extra mile.' Why? What's there? More work? For the same money?"

**Action:** Counting: one finger, two fingers, slight exasperation

---

### Shot 4: Nan Comparison (0:28-0:38)

**Visual:** Warm close-up, nostalgic expression

**Audio:** "My nan worked 9 to 5. She went home. To her family. To her life. She didn't answer emails at 8 PM. She didn't have emails. She had tea."

**Action:** Warm smile, holds tea mug with both hands

---

### Shot 5: Team Player (0:38-0:48)

**Visual:** Medium shot, slightly annoyed

**Audio:** "'Be a team player.' That means cover for the people they didn't hire. 'Wear many hats.' One salary. Five jobs."

**Action:** Mimes putting on multiple hats, deadpan

---

### Shot 6: The Truth (0:48-0:58)

**Visual:** Close-up, firm expression

**Audio:** "Quiet quitting isn't quitting. It's working your contracted hours. And calling it a day. I log off at 5 PM. My phone stays in my bag. My emails wait until tomorrow."

**Action:** Mimes logging off, putting phone away

---

### Shot 7: Outro (0:58-1:05)

**Visual:** Medium shot, standing up slightly

**Audio:** "Am I quiet quitting? Or am I loudly working my contracted hours? Your hustle culture is my 'I have a life.' Rant over. Going home. It's 5 PM."

**Action:** Looks at imaginary watch, stands, walks off
