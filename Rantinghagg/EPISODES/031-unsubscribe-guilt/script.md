# Script: I Can't Unsubscribe (What If I Need It?)

**Episode:** 031  
**Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Format:** TikTok/Reels/Shorts  
**Character:** RantingHagg (British woman, 68-72, green cardigan, tea mug)

---

## 🎬 SHOT-BY-SHOT SCRIPT

### Shot 1: Opening - Unsubscribe (0:00-0:08)

**Visual:** Medium shot, Hagg in armchair, looking guilty

**Audio:** "I can't unsubscribe. What if I need it? I haven't watched Disney+ in 8 months. But what if I need it? For what? I don't know."

**Action:** Shrugs, confused expression, sip of tea

---

### Shot 2: Free Trial Trap (0:08-0:18)

**Visual:** Close-up, frustrated

**Audio:** "'Start your free trial!' they say. 'Cancel anytime!' they lie. 17 clicks later, I'm still paying."

**Action:** Holds up fingers, counts to 17, frustrated

---

### Shot 3: Its Only 999 (0:18-0:28)

**Visual:** Medium shot, counting on fingers

**Audio:** "'It's only £9.99.' It's never just £9.99. It's £9.99 here, £12.99 there, £7.99 everywhere. Suddenly I'm paying £200 a month. For what?"

**Action:** Three fingers, then shocked at £200

---

### Shot 4: Subscription Count (0:28-0:38)

**Visual:** Close-up, ashamed

**Audio:** "I have 12 subscriptions. I use three. I cancel none. Sunk subscription fallacy: 'I've paid this long. Might as well continue.'"

**Action:** Holds up 12 fingers (joking), then ashamed look

---

### Shot 5: Nan Comparison (0:38-0:48)

**Visual:** Warm close-up, nostalgic

**Audio:** "My nan subscribed to one thing: the Radio Times. She read it. Every week. Cover to cover. She knew what was on. She watched three shows."

**Action:** Warm smile, mimes reading magazine

---

### Shot 6: Cancellation Hell (0:48-0:58)

**Visual:** Medium shot, frustrated

**Audio:** "Cancelling a subscription should not require a phone call. In 2026. 'Press 1 for sales. Press 2 for... actually, can I just cancel online?' No."

**Action:** Mimes pressing buttons, increasingly frustrated

---

### Shot 7: Outro (0:58-1:05)

**Visual:** Medium shot, determined

**Audio:** "Rant over. Cancelling Disney+. Still haven't watched it. But I'm cancelling. Today. Probably."

**Action:** Nods determined, then "probably" uncertainty

---

## 🎵 AUDIO NOTES

- **Background:** Light acoustic, slightly frustrated tone
- **Pacing:** Moderate, building frustration
- **Emphasis:** "8 months," "17 clicks," "£200," "12 subscriptions," "probably"
