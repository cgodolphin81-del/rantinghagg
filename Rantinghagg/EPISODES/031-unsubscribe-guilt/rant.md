# I Can't Unsubscribe (What If I Need It?)

**Episode:** 031  
**Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Status:** Ready for script

---

I can't unsubscribe. What if I need it?

I haven't watched Disney+ in 8 months. But what if I need it? For what? I don't know. The uncertainty is keeping me subscribed.

"Start your free trial!" they say. "Cancel anytime!" they lie.

17 clicks later, I'm still paying.

"It's only £9.99." It's never just £9.99.

It's £9.99 here, £12.99 there, £7.99 everywhere. Suddenly I'm paying £200 a month. For what?

I have 12 subscriptions. I use three. I cancel none.

Sunk subscription fallacy: "I've paid this long. Might as well continue."

You've paid. That money's gone. Continuing is throwing more money after it.

My nan subscribed to one thing: the Radio Times. She read it. Every week. Cover to cover. She knew what was on. She watched three shows.

I have Netflix, Prime, Disney+, Apple+, NowTV, Paramount, Discovery... I watch BBC iPlayer. It's free.

Cancelling a subscription should not require a phone call. In 2026.

"Press 1 for sales. Press 2 for... actually, can I just cancel online?" No.

Rant over. Cancelling Disney+. Still haven't watched it. But I'm cancelling. Today. Probably.
