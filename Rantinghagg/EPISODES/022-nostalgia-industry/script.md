# Video Script: Nostalgic For A Time I Didn't Even Like

*[looks at camera]*

## [0:10-0:25] THE REBOOT

**HAGG:**
And I'm buying a "vintage" t-shirt. From 2019.

Which is new.

But it's "vintage."

Because nostalgia is a product.

And you're the product.

---

## [0:25-0:40] THE MOVIES

**HAGG:**
Every movie is a reboot now.

A sequel. A prequel. A remake.

They're not making new stories.

They're making old stories.

With bigger budgets.

And better CGI.

---

## [0:40-0:55] THE MUSIC

**HAGG:**
And the music.

Everything sounds the same.

Because algorithms decide what's good.

And algorithms want sameness.

So everything sounds like the last thing that worked.

---

## [0:55-1:10] THE TRUTH

**HAGG:**
I'm nostalgic for a time. I didn't even like.

When I was there.

I'm not missing that time.

I'm missing the person I was.

In that time.

Who didn't know better.

---

## [1:10-1:15] OUTRO

**HAGG:**
Nostalgia isn't memory anymore.

It's a product.

And you're the product.

Rant over.

*[sips tea. Fade out.]*
