# Return To Office (We Miss Your Money)

**Episode:** 029  
**Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Status:** Ready for script

---

Return to office. They call it "collaboration."

It's not collaboration. It's the lease.

They signed 10-year deals. Now nobody's there. Empty offices. Empty desks. Empty expensive coffee machines.

"I miss the office." No you don't. You miss the commute? The open plan? The bad coffee?

Collaboration is a calendar invite. I can do that at home.

"Company culture." Culture is awkward small talk at the lift. "How was your weekend?" "Fine." "Mine too." Back to desk.

"Junior staff need mentoring." Mentoring is watching someone else work. At home. On Zoom. Same thing.

My commute is 90 minutes. Each way. Three hours a day. For what?

I could work 8 hours at home. Plus 3 hours of life.

They want me back for "culture." I want my life back.

"Stronger together." Stronger apart. On my sofa. In my pants.

My nan worked from home. It was called "the house." She didn't commute. She walked downstairs.

Return to office is "we miss your money" in corporate speak.

I'm not coming back for your hot desking. Where my desk is someone else's.

Rant over. Working from home. Still in my pants.
