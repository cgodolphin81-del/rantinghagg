```

```

### Scene 4: The Friendship (0:40-0:55)

```
Close-up, woman leans forward, 
"Making friends as an adult?" with slight laugh, 
"It's like dating. But worse." (shakes head)

"And hoping. And being rejected." (sad)
"But it's not called rejection." (slight scoff)
"It's called 'they're busy.'" (deadpan)
lighting: intimate, key light from side
```

### Scene 5: Third Places (0:55-1:10)

```
Medium shot, woman's expression shifts to questioning, 
"Work from home." with slight shrug, 
"Until you realise you haven't spoken to another human." (pauses)
"In three days." (lets it land)
"Except the delivery driver." (slight laugh)
"And that's the highlight of your week." (resigned)
"Third places are dead." (serious)
"Pubs are closing. Cafes are too expensive." (counts)
"Where do you just... be?" (quiet)
lighting: slightly darker
```

### Scene 6: Nan Comparison (1:10-1:20)

```
Medium shot, woman's expression warms, 
"My nan had friends." with warmth, 
"Proper friends." (nods)
"From work. From the neighborhood. From the WI." (counts)
"She'd see them every week." (smiles)
"She'd have a cuppa. She'd have a natter." (mimes)
"She'd never be alone." (warmth)
lighting: golden hour glow
```

### Scene 7: Outro (1:20-1:30)

```
Close-up, woman sets mug down slowly, 
"I have 500 followers." with resignation, 
"And I'm so lonely I'm starting to talk to my plants." (slight laugh)
"And they're not great conversationalists." (deadpan)
"They just... sit there. Judging me." (slight smile)
"For not watering them enough." (small laugh)
"We're the most connected generation." (pauses)
```
