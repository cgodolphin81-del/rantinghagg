# Self-Care Is Just A Marketing Term

**Episode:** 039  
**Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Status:** Ready for script

---

Self-care used to mean sleep.

Now it means £45 face cream.

"Treat yourself."

"You deserve it."

"Self-care Sunday."

"Invest in yourself."

"Treat yourself" = buy something.

"You deserve it" = spend money.

"Self-care Sunday" = £200 spa day.

"Invest in yourself" = £500 course.

Self-care market: $546 billion.

70% of self-care posts are product promotions.

Real self-care = sleep, rest, boundaries.

Not £300 shopping spree.

The algorithm wants you stressed.

Then sells you self-care.

"You're stressed! Buy this!"

"You're tired! Buy this!"

"You're anxious! Buy this!"

My nan's self-care was a sit-down.

And a biscuit.

Cost: 20p.

Worked.

Rant over. Having a sit-down. And a biscuit.
