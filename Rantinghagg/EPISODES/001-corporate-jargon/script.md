## [0:00-0:08] OPENING

*[Hagg in armchair, adjusts glasses, looks genuinely pained]*

**HAGG:**
I was in a meeting yesterday. A "sync," if you will.

*[air quotes with fingers]*

Someone said — and I quote — "synergistic alignment of our core competencies to leverage low-hanging fruit."

*[stares at camera, deadpan]*

## [0:08-0:20] THE HOOK

**HAGG:**
We are living in a corporate jargon death loop.

*[sips tea, sets mug down firmly]*

Before we all "onboard" onto a ship that's clearly sinking.

## [0:20-0:40] THE RANT

**HAGG:**
Why can't we just say... "Let's use what we're good at to sell more stuff soon"?

*[leans forward]*

No. That's too simple. Doesn't sound "strategic."

We have to "architect a paradigm shift." We have to "disrupt the ecosystem."

*[counts on fingers, increasingly exasperated]*

We have to "think outside the box" while simultaneously "staying in our lane."

*[throws hands up]*

It's exhausting.

---

## [0:40-0:55] THE KILLER LINE

**HAGG:**
"Circle back." You know what that means?

*[leans in, conspiratorial]*

"I have no idea what you just asked, and I'm hoping you forget about it by Tuesday."

*[sips tea triumphantly]*

---

## [0:55-1:10] THE PAYOFF

**HAGG:**
And "low-hanging fruit." What is this, a community orchard?

Are we all dressed in overalls? Carrying baskets?

*[looks down at own cardigan, tea mug]*

No. We're in Dubai. Drinking coffee that costs more than a small car.

Pretending that "sending an email"... is a harvest.

---

## [1:10-1:20] OUTRO

**HAGG:**
Here's my actionable insight.

*[direct to camera]*

If anyone asks you to "ideate"... just bark at them.

*[sips tea]*

It's the only logical response.

Rant over. I'm taking this offline.

*[waves dismissively. Fade out.]*
