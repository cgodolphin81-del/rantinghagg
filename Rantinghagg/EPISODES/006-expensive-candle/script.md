# Video Script: The Expensive Candle

**Episode:** 006  
**Duration:** 75-90 seconds  
**Format:** TikTok / Reels / Shorts  
**Voice:** RantingHagg (British, dry, incredulous)

---

## [0:00-0:10] OPENING

*[Hagg holding an expensive-looking candle, examining it]*

**HAGG:**
Right. Forty-seven pounds.

*[looks at camera]*

For a candle.

*[gestures]*

Not a chandelier. Not a lamp. A candle.

A bit of wax. With a string in it.

---

## [0:10-0:25] THE HOOK

**HAGG:**
And it doesn't even smell like something specific.

*[sniffs candle, confused]*

It smells like... "Autumn Nostalgia."

*[pauses]*

What does that mean? That's not a smell. That's a feeling.

*[sets candle down]*

You can't put a feeling in a jar.

*[sips tea]*

I've tried. It's called therapy.

---

## [0:25-0:40] REALITY CHECK

**HAGG:**
You know what autumn actually smells like?

*[counts on fingers]*

Damp leaves. Slightly mouldy conkers. That weird smell when you turn the heating on for the first time...

*[mimes sniffing]*

...and it burns off six months of dust.

*[nods]*

THAT'S autumn nostalgia. And it's free.

*[points off-screen]*

Go outside.

---

## [0:40-0:55] HAND-POURED

**HAGG:**
But no. We've got "hand-poured" candles now.

*[air quotes]*

As opposed to what?

*[genuinely curious]*

Foot-poured? Machine-gunned into the jar?

*[sips tea]*

Of course it's hand-poured, Karen. That's how pouring works.

---

## [0:55-1:10] SUSTAINABLE

**HAGG:**
"Made with sustainable soy wax and essential oils."

*[pauses]*

It's a candle. It burns.

*[gestures with tea]*

That's literally the opposite of sustainable. You're setting it on fire. On purpose.

---

## [1:10-1:25] THE MATH

**HAGG:**
And the best bit? It burns for thirty hours.

So you're paying forty-seven quid... for thirty hours...

*[does mental math]*

...of pretending your living room is a boutique hotel.

That's one pound fifty-seven an hour.

*[nods]*

To smell like someone else's idea of a memory.

---

## [1:25-1:35] NAN COMPARISON

**HAGG:**
My nan had a candle.

*[warm smile]*

It was white. It cost forty pence.

*[sips tea]*

It smelled like candle.

And we were grateful.

---

## [1:35-1:40] OUTRO

**HAGG:**
Rant over.

*[picks up candle]*

I'm going to light this. And think about what I've done.

*[exhales. Fade out.]*
