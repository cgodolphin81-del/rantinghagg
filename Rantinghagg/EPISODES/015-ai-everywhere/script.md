# Video Script: My Therapist Is An Algorithm

**Episode:** 015  
**Duration:** 75-90 seconds  
**Format:** TikTok / Reels / Shorts  
**Voice:** RantingHagg (British, dry, lonely but warm)

---

## [0:00-0:10] OPENING

*[Hagg in armchair, holding phone, looking at it]*

**HAGG:**
Your therapist is an algorithm.

*[looks at camera]*

And it's cheaper.

You're talking to a chatbot. About your feelings.

The chatbot doesn't have feelings.

But it pretends. And that's enough.

---

## [0:10-0:25] THE FRIENDS

**HAGG:**
Because your actual friends don't ask.

They're too busy. Or they don't care.

Or they're also talking to chatbots.

*"AI wrote my breakup text."*

Good. At least someone cared enough. To outsource it.

---

## [0:25-0:40] THE ART

**HAGG:**
"AI art killed my job."

Did it?

Or did it just kill the part of you. That pretended art was a career?

*[sips tea]*

Everything's AI now.

Your toaster has AI. Your fridge has AI. Your dating app has AI.

---

## [0:40-0:55] THE JOB

**HAGG:**
Your job application is read by AI.

Your CV is written by AI.

You're being interviewed by AI.

And rejected by AI.

And you don't even know.

*"AI-powered."* It says that on everything.

---

## [0:55-1:10] THE LIE

**HAGG:**
Even when it's not.

Because AI sells.

Even when the AI is just... a dropdown menu. With extra steps.

And you can't tell what's real anymore.

Is that photo real? Is that person real? Is that post real?

Or did an algorithm write it?

---

## [1:10-1:25] NAN'S LETTERS

**HAGG:**
My nan wrote letters. With a pen. On paper.

And she posted them.

And someone got them. Days later.

And they read them. And they kept them.

You send a text. It's read in three seconds.

And deleted. And forgotten.

And the AI wrote it.

---

## [1:25-1:30] OUTRO

**HAGG:**
At least the AI pretends to care.

Unlike my actual friends.

Rant over.

*[fade out on her looking at phone]*
