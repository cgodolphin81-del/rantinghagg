# Wellness For People Who Have Money

**Episode:** 034  
**Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Status:** Ready for script

Wellness is just rich people self-care. With better marketing.

£500 for a meditation retreat. I can't afford £50 for a dentist.

But I should sound bath. For my chakras.

Influencer self-care: £300 spa day. My self-care: Cup of tea. 50p.

Both relax. One costs 600 times more.

" Wake up at 4 AM. Meditate. Green juice. Yoga."

My wellness: Wake at 6. Tired. Tea. Sit down.

Both achieve: Not dying.

Can't afford heating. But should meditate. For my chakras.

I'm cold. But I should breathe. I'm stressed about money. But I should do yoga.

At 6 AM. Before my 4 jobs.

Wellness industry is £7 trillion. For what? Selling you back your own relaxation. At 600x markup.

My nan's wellness was a sit-down. And a biscuit. Cost: 20p. Worked.

"Buy this £200 cushion. For your meditation."

I'll buy a £20 cushion. And a £180 cup of tea.

Wellness for poor people: Walk in the park. Free. But it's raining.

Wellness for rich: £5,000 retreat. In the sun. With crystals.

Rant over. Having a cuppa. 50p. My chakras are fine.
