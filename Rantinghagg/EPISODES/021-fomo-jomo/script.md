# Video Script: FOMO About My JOMO

**Episode:** 021  
**Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Format:** TikTok / Reels / Shorts  
**Voice:** RantingHagg (British, dry, amused)

---

## [0:00-0:10] OPENING

*[Hagg in armchair, holding phone, looking conflicted]*

**HAGG:**
I have FOMO.

About my JOMO.

*[pauses]*

I'm missing out. On missing out.

---

## [0:10-0:25] THE FOMO

**HAGG:**
FOMO is anxiety.

You're at home. And you're wondering.

What are they doing? Without me.

Are they having fun? Without me.

Did they invite me? And I said no.

And now I regret it.

---

## [0:25-0:40] THE JOMO

**HAGG:**
JOMO is the Joy Of Missing Out.

It's staying in. And pretending you don't care.

It's "I'm so glad I didn't go."

While you're scrolling. Through pictures.

Of them. Going. Without you.

And you're not glad.

You're lonely.

With better branding.

---

## [0:40-0:55] THE CHOICE

**HAGG:**
You can't win.

If you go out: FOMO about what you're missing at home.

If you stay in: FOMO about what you're missing out there.

Anxious when going out.

Anxious when staying in.

Can't just... exist.

Without comparing.

---

## [0:55-1:10] THE TRUTH

**HAGG:**
Social media made both worse.

Because now you can see.

Exactly what you're missing.

In real time.

With filters.

---

## [1:10-1:15] OUTRO

**HAGG:**
I have FOMO about my JOMO.

I'm missing out. On missing out.

And I'm exhausted.

Rant over.

*[sips tea. Stays home. Fade out.]*
