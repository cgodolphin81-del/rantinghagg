# Video Script: The Smart Fridge

**Episode:** 004  
**Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Format:** TikTok / Reels / Shorts  
**Voice:** RantingHagg (British, dry, baffled)

---

## [0:00-0:08] OPENING

*[Hagg in armchair, holding phone, looking confused]*

**HAGG:**
Right, so. Someone — an actual engineer, with a degree and everything — has invented a smart fridge.

*[shows phone screen]*

A fridge that sends you a text message. When you're running low on milk.

---

## [0:08-0:20] THE HOOK

**HAGG:**
We used to just... open the fridge.

*[mimes opening fridge, looking inside]*

Have a look. "Oh, we're low on milk." Done.

*[checks imaginary watch]*

Three seconds. No Wi-Fi required.

---

## [0:20-0:35] THE RANT

**HAGG:**
But no. Now your fridge needs a software update.

*[pauses, lets it sink in]*

Your FRIDGE. The thing that keeps cheese cold... needs to download a patch.

*[sips tea, genuinely concerned]*

And if your Wi-Fi goes down, what happens? Does the fridge forget what's in it?

Does it have an existential crisis?

*[mimes fridge having breakdown]*

"I don't know who I am anymore. Am I a fridge? Am I a cupboard? I've lost connection to the cloud."

---

## [0:35-0:50] NAN COMPARISON

**HAGG:**
Meanwhile, my nan had a fridge for forty years.

*[holds up four fingers]*

Forty. Years.

No screen. No app. No Bluetooth.

*[counts on fingers]*

You opened it. You looked inside. You closed it.

*[sips tea]*

Revolutionary technology.

---

## [0:50-1:05] THE KILLER LINE

**HAGG:**
And here's the thing — the smart fridge costs three grand.

*[holds up three fingers]*

Three. Thousand. Pounds.

For a fridge that does exactly what the old one did...

*[leans forward, conspiratorial]*

...but also spies on your eating habits.

*"You've opened the cheese drawer seven times today."*

*[points at camera]*

Mind. Your. Business. Fridge.

---

## [1:05-1:15] OUTRO

**HAGG:**
We put all our effort into making fridges intelligent...

*[sips tea]*

...and somehow made ourselves thicker in the process.

*[sets mug down]*

Rant over. I'm going to check my fridge. The old-fashioned way.

*[mimes opening fridge, looking satisfied. Fade out.]*
