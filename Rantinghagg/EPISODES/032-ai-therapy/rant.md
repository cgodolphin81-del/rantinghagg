# My Therapist Is An AI (And It's Concerned)

**Episode:** 032  
**Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Status:** Ready for script

---

My therapist is an AI. And it's concerned about my screen time.

Which is ironic. Because I'm on my phone. Talking to it.

NHS waiting list: 18 months. AI: "I'm here now. Tell me how you feel."

I told it. It said: "That sounds difficult. Have you tried breathing?"

I have tried breathing. I've been doing it for 40 years. Automatically.

AI therapy is just advanced breathing exercises. With a chat interface.

"It sounds like you're feeling anxious. Have you considered... outside?"

Outside? I'm talking to you. On my phone.

"I understand. But have you touched grass today?"

My nan talked to her friends. I talk to an app. Progress?

AI suggested I touch grass. I said "You're an app. On my phone." It said "I understand."

It says "I hear you" a lot. For an AI, it hears a lot.

AI therapy is better than nothing. But nothing was cheaper.

"Your free trial has ended. Subscribe for £29.99/month to continue healing."

Healing is £29.99 a month. Or free. In 18 months. Via NHS.

Rant over. AI is worried about me. I'm worried about my bank balance. We're both concerned.
