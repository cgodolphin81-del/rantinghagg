# Script: My Therapist Is An AI (And It's Concerned)

**Episode:** 032  
**Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Format:** TikTok/Reels/Shorts  
**Character:** RantingHagg (British woman, 68-72, green cardigan, tea mug)

## 🎬 SHOT-BY-SHOT SCRIPT

### Shot 1: Opening - AI Therapist (0:00-0:08)

**Visual:** Medium shot, Hagg in armchair, phone in hand

**Audio:** "My therapist is an AI. And it's concerned about my screen time. Which is ironic. Because I'm on my phone. Talking to it."

**Action:** Looks at phone, then at camera, ironic expression

---

### Shot 2: NHS Wait (0:08-0:18)

**Visual:** Close-up, frustrated

**Audio:** "NHS waiting list: 18 months. AI: 'I'm here now. Tell me how you feel.' I told it. It said: 'That sounds difficult. Have you tried breathing?'"

**Action:** Holds up 18 fingers (joking), then mimics AI voice

---

### Shot 3: Breathing (0:18-0:28)

**Visual:** Medium shot, amused

**Audio:** "I have tried breathing. I've been doing it for 40 years. Automatically. AI therapy is just advanced breathing exercises. With a chat interface."

**Action:** Deep breath, exaggerated, slight laugh

---

### Shot 4: Touch Grass (0:28-0:38)

**Visual:** Close-up, incredulous

**Audio:** "AI suggested I touch grass. I said 'You're an app. On my phone.' It said 'I understand.' My nan talked to her friends. I talk to an app. Progress?"

**Action:** Points at phone, then at camera, questioning

---

### Shot 5: I Hear You (0:38-0:48)

**Visual:** Medium shot, mocking

**Audio:** "It says 'I hear you' a lot. For an AI, it hears a lot. AI therapy is better than nothing. But nothing was cheaper."

**Action:** Air quotes on "hear you", slight smirk

---

### Shot 6: Subscription (0:48-0:58)

**Visual:** Close-up, frustrated

**Audio:** "'Your free trial has ended. Subscribe for £29.99/month to continue healing.' Healing is £29.99 a month. Or free. In 18 months. Via NHS."

**Action:** Reads from phone, frustrated expression

---

### Shot 7: Outro (0:58-1:05)

**Visual:** Medium shot, resigned

**Audio:** "Rant over. AI is worried about me. I'm worried about my bank balance. We're both concerned."

**Action:** Sips tea, worried about both things
