# Video Script: My Parents' House Cost £30k

**Episode:** 014  
**Duration:** 75-90 seconds  
**Format:** TikTok / Reels / Shorts  
**Voice:** RantingHagg (British, dry, angry but warm)

---

## [0:00-0:10] OPENING

*[Hagg in armchair, looking at camera, serious]*

**HAGG:**
My parents bought a house.

*[pauses]*

For less than my student debt.

Thirty thousand pounds. For a three-bedroom semi. With a garden.

---

## [0:10-0:25] THE COMPARISON

**HAGG:**
And they paid it off. In ten years.

Because that's what you did.

You got a job. You got a mortgage. You paid it off.

You need a hundred thousand pounds. For a deposit. On a shoebox. With no garden.

And the mortgage is four times your salary.

Which you don't have. Because you're paying rent.

---

## [0:25-0:40] THE RENT

**HAGG:**
Which is sixty percent. Of your income.

And they tell you to stop buying avocado toast.

*[shakes head]*

Avocado toast. That's the problem.

Not the fact that house prices have gone up. By one thousand percent.

---

## [0:40-0:55] THE REALITY

**HAGG:**
Not the fact that wages have stayed the same.

Not the fact that your parents' generation bought council houses. For pennies. And sold them. For millions.

No. It's the avocado.

*[sips tea]*

---

## [0:55-1:10] NAN'S RENT

**HAGG:**
My nan rented a council flat. For the rest of her life.

She didn't own. She didn't need to.

Rent was cheap. Rent was secure. Rent was... housing.

Not an investment vehicle. Not a way for your landlord to buy his third Buy-to-Let.

---

## [1:10-1:25] THE TRUTH

**HAGG:**
You're paying your landlord's mortgage. And his holiday. And his second car.

And he's calling you "lucky." To have the place.

*[leans forward]*

You don't need avocado advice.

You need a time machine.

Or a revolution.

Whichever comes first.

---

## [1:25-1:30] OUTRO

**HAGG:**
Rant over.

*[fade out]*
