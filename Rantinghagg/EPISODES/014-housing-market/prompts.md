### Scene 1: Opening - Student Debt (0:00-0:10)

"For less than my student debt." with slight laugh,

lighting: warm, intimate
```

### Scene 2: The Comparison (0:10-0:25)

```
Close-up, woman counts on fingers, 
"Thirty thousand pounds." (holds up three fingers)
"For a three-bedroom semi. With a garden." (smiles)
"And they paid it off. In ten years." (nods)
"Because that's what you did." (simple shrug)
"You got a job. You got a mortgage. You paid it off." (counts)
lighting: nostalgic golden hour
```

### Scene 3: The Reality (0:25-0:40)

```
Medium shot, woman's expression shifts to concern, 
"You need a hundred thousand pounds." (shocked)
"For a deposit." (emphasizes)
"On a shoebox. With no garden." (shakes head)
"And the mortgage is four times your salary." (serious)
"Which you don't have." (empathetic)
"Because you're paying rent." (slight anger)
lighting: slightly dramatic
```

### Scene 4: The Rent (0:40-0:55)

```
Close-up, woman leans forward, 
"Which is sixty percent. Of your income." with disbelief, 
"And they tell you to stop buying avocado toast." with slight scoff, 
"Avocado toast. That's the problem." (deadpan)
"Not the fact that house prices have gone up." (counts)
"By one thousand percent." (lets it land)
lighting: key light from side
```

### Scene 5: The Truth (0:55-1:10)

```
Medium shot, woman's expression shifts to slight anger, 
"Not the fact that wages have stayed the same." (serious)
"Not the fact that your parents' generation..." (builds)
"Bought council houses. For pennies." (emphasizes)
"And sold them. For millions." (slight anger)
"No. It's the avocado." (deadpan)
sips tea, lighting: warm but firm
```

### Scene 6: Nan's Rent (1:10-1:25)

```
Medium shot, woman's expression softens, 
"My nan rented a council flat." with nostalgia, 
"For the rest of her life." (nods)
"She didn't own. She didn't need to." (simple)
"Rent was cheap. Rent was secure." (warmth)
"Rent was... housing." (emphasizes)
"Not an investment vehicle." (slight anger)
"Not a way for your landlord to buy his third Buy-to-Let." (head shake)
lighting: golden hour glow
```

### Scene 7: The Payoff (1:25-1:35)

```
Close-up, woman leans forward, 
"You're paying your landlord's mortgage." (serious)
"And his holiday. And his second car." (counts)
"And he's calling you 'lucky'." (slight scoff)
"To have the place." (shakes head)
pauses, "You don't need avocado advice." (firm)
"You need a time machine." (slight smile)
"Or a revolution." (warmer smile)
"Whichever comes first." (small laugh)
lighting: warm, hopeful
```

### Scene 8: Outro (1:35-1:40)

```
Medium shot, woman sips tea, 
"Rant over." with small nod, 
fade out, 
lighting: warm fade
