# Video Script: Monetize Your Hobby (Now You Hate It)

**Episode:** 020  
**Duration:** 75-90 seconds  
**Format:** TikTok / Reels / Shorts  
**Voice:** RantingHagg (British, dry, slightly sad)

---

## [0:00-0:10] OPENING

*[Hagg in armchair, holding knitting, looking at it]*

**HAGG:**
I knit one scarf.

*[looks at camera]*

Now I have an Etsy shop.

And an Instagram.

And a podcast. About knitting.

---

## [0:10-0:25] THE PRESSURE

**HAGG:**
And I'm burnt out.

And I hate knitting.

Because everything has to make money now.

You can't just knit.

For fun.

You have to monetize.

You have to build a brand.

You have to have a side hustle.

---

## [0:25-0:40] THE SIDE HUSTLE

**HAGG:**
"Side hustle" used to mean:

"You do a bit of freelance. On the side."

Now it means:

"Your hobby isn't valid. Unless it makes money."

"You're not trying hard enough. Unless you're monetizing."

"You're wasting your life. Unless it's profitable."

---

## [0:40-0:55] THE PASSIVE INCOME

**HAGG:**
And "passive income." Oh, "passive income."

It's not passive.

It's active. It's exhausting.

It's checking your Etsy stats at 2 AM.

It's packaging orders. It's customer service.

It's posting on Instagram. When you'd rather be knitting.

---

## [0:55-1:10] NAN COMPARISON

**HAGG:**
My nan knitted.

For fun.

She didn't have an Etsy shop.

She didn't have a podcast.

She had a jumper.

And she was warm.

And she was happy.

---

## [1:10-1:20] THE TRUTH

**HAGG:**
Some things are just hobbies.

Let them be hobbies.

Don't monetize everything.

Don't optimize everything.

Don't turn your joy. Into a business.

---

## [1:20-1:25] OUTRO

**HAGG:**
My side hustle is my main hustle now.

And I hate it.

Rant over.

I'm going to knit.

For fun.

*[starts knitting. Fade out.]*
