# Script: My Manager Does Less Than Me

**Episode:** 028  
**Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Format:** TikTok/Reels/Shorts  
**Character:** RantingHagg (British woman, 68-72, green cardigan, tea mug)

---

## 🎬 SHOT-BY-SHOT SCRIPT

### Shot 1: Opening - Manager (0:00-0:08)

**Visual:** Medium shot, Hagg in armchair, looking frustrated

**Audio:** "My manager does less than me. And earns triple. I do the work. She schedules meetings about the work."

**Action:** Slight eye roll, sip of tea

---

### Shot 2: Strategic (0:08-0:18)

**Visual:** Close-up, air quotes

**Audio:** "Her biggest achievement? Delegating my achievement. 'Strategic.' That's corporate for 'I don't do anything.'"

**Action:** Air quotes on "strategic", deadpan

---

### Shot 3: Meeting Translation (0:18-0:28)

**Visual:** Medium shot, counting on fingers

**Audio:** "'I'm working strategically.' Means: I'm in meetings all day. 'Let's align.' Means: I need to talk about talking. 'Big picture.' Means: I don't know the details."

**Action:** Three fingers, counting each translation

---

### Shot 4: Meeting Hours (0:28-0:38)

**Visual:** Close-up, incredulous

**Audio:** "My manager spends 35 hours a week in meetings. I spend 35 hours doing the work she meets about. She gets promoted. For managing. Which is not-working. With extra steps."

**Action:** Mimes "extra steps" with fingers

---

### Shot 5: Promotion Paradox (0:38-0:48)

**Visual:** Medium shot, shaking head

**Audio:** "Best worker gets promoted. Now they don't work. They manage. It's a paradox. Work well. Get promoted to not-work."

**Action:** Shakes head, confused expression

---

### Shot 6: Nan Comparison (0:48-0:58)

**Visual:** Warm close-up, nostalgic

**Audio:** "My nan worked. She made things. You could see what she did. A jumper. A cake. A clean house. My manager makes... slides. About work. That I do."

**Action:** Warm smile, then slight disgust at "slides"

---

### Shot 7: Outro (0:58-1:05)

**Visual:** Medium shot, back to work

**Audio:** "Rant over. Back to work. She's in a meeting. About my work."

**Action:** Turns back to imaginary desk, resigned

---

- **Pacing:** Moderate, building frustration
- **Emphasis:** "triple," "strategic," "35 hours," "not-working"
