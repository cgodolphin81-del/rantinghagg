# My Manager Does Less Than Me

**Episode:** 028  
**Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Status:** Ready for script

---

My manager does less than me. And earns triple.

I do the work. She schedules meetings about the work.

Her biggest achievement? Delegating my achievement.

"Strategic." That's corporate for "I don't do anything."

"I'm working strategically." Means: I'm in meetings all day.

"Let's align." Means: I need to talk about talking.

"Big picture." Means: I don't know the details.

My manager spends 35 hours a week in meetings. I spend 35 hours doing the work she meets about.

She gets promoted. For managing. Which is not-working. With extra steps.

Best worker gets promoted. Now they don't work. They manage.

It's a paradox. Work well. Get promoted to not-work.

My nan worked. She made things. You could see what she did. A jumper. A cake. A clean house.

My manager makes... slides. About work. That I do.

Her job title: "VP of Strategic Solutions." What she does: Schedules meetings.

My job title: "Associate." What I do: Everything.

Pay gap: She earns triple. For less.

This is not a meritocracy. This is a meeting-ocracy.

Rant over. Back to work. She's in a meeting. About my work.
