# Grindset Is For People Who Hate Themselves

**Episode:** 027

Grindset is for people who hate themselves.

Who told you suffering was virtuous?

"Wake up at 4 AM." Why? What's happening at 4 AM? The sun's not even up. The birds aren't up. Why are you up?

"Sleep when you're dead." I'd rather sleep before. Thanks.

My nan worked hard. She also had tea. And naps. And Sundays off. She didn't have a "morning routine." She had breakfast.

The 4 AM club is a cult. With merch.

"Hustle harder." For what? So I can afford a house in 2080?

"Multiple income streams." I want one income. That pays properly.

Your side hustle is my "I'm exhausted."

Your passive income is my "I'm checking Etsy stats at 2 AM."

Grindset guys at 40: "Why am I divorced and alone?"

Because you grinded. And hustled. And missed everything.

I'm not hustling. I'm working. Then I'm living.

My nan's hustle was grinding tea bags. Into submission. She didn't call it hustle. She called it tea.

Sleep when you're dead? I'm sleeping now. At 10 PM. Like a sensible person.

Your grindset is my "I have a life."

Rant over. Going to bed. It's 10 PM. I'm not hustling. I'm sleeping.
