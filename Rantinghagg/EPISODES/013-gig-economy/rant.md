# Be Your Own Boss (Unemployed)

**Episode:** 013  
**Category:** Gig Economy  
**Source:** Content Calendar + Research  
**Status:** Ready for script

---

You're not self-employed. You're unemployed. With an app.

"Be your own boss." That's what they tell you. What it actually means is: you have no boss, no pay, and no rights. But you do have an app. That tells you where to go. When to go. And how much you'll get. Which is nothing. After expenses.

You're delivering food. For three pounds an hour. After petrol. After car wear. After tax. You're paying to work. That's not a job. That's a hobby. That costs money.

And the algorithm. The algorithm is your boss now. And it doesn't care if you're sick. If your car breaks down. If it's raining. If you're tired. The algorithm just... assigns. And you accept. Or you don't eat.

"Flexible." That's the word they use. Flexible means you're always available. Flexible means you work when the app says. Flexible means no holidays. No sick pay. No nothing.

My nan had a job. She clocked in. She clocked out. She got paid. Every week. Like clockwork. She didn't have an algorithm. She had a boss. And the boss was a person. A person who knew her name.

You have an app. And the app doesn't know you exist.

You're not self-employed. You're unemployed. With an app. And the gig is losing.
