## 🎬 SCENE PROMPTS (Video Generation)

### Scene 1: Opening - Unemployed (0:00-0:10)

```
Medium shot, British woman 65-75 in armchair, 
looking at camera seriously, 
says "You're not self-employed" with conviction, 
pauses, "You're unemployed. With an app." with slight head shake, 
warm living room, bookshelves, rainy window, 
lighting: warm but serious
```

### Scene 2: The Reality (0:10-0:25)

```
Close-up, woman counts on fingers, 
"No boss. No pay. And no rights." (three fingers)
"But you do have an app." (holds up phone)
"That tells you where to go." (points)
"When to go. And how much you'll get." (shrugs)
"Which is nothing. After expenses." with resignation, 
lighting: slightly dramatic
```

### Scene 3: The Math (0:25-0:40)

```
Medium shot, woman's expression shifts to disbelief, 
"Three pounds an hour" with shock, 
"After petrol. After car wear. After tax." (counts)
"You're paying. To work." with head shake, 
"That's not a job. That's a hobby." (slight laugh)
"That costs money." with sip of tea, 
lighting: warm, consistent
```

### Scene 4: The Algorithm (0:40-0:55)

```
Close-up, woman leans forward, 
"The algorithm is your boss now." with seriousness, 
"And it doesn't care if you're sick." (shakes head)
"If your car breaks down. If it's raining." (counts)
"If you're tired." (empathetic)
"The algorithm just... assigns." (mimes phone)
"And you accept. Or you don't eat." (serious)
lighting: key light from side, dramatic
```

### Scene 5: The Lie (0:55-1:10)

```
Medium shot, woman's expression shifts to slight anger, 
"Flexible." with air quotes, 
"That's the word they use." with slight scoff, 
"Flexible means you're always available." (serious)
"No holidays. No sick pay. No nothing." (counts)
lighting: warm but firm
```

### Scene 6: Nan Comparison (1:10-1:25)

```
Medium shot, woman's expression softens, 
"My nan had a job." with warmth, 
"She clocked in. She clocked out. She got paid." (nods)
"Every week. Like clockwork." (satisfied)
"She had a boss. And the boss was a person." (emphasizes)
"A person who knew her name." (warm smile)
"You have an app. And the app doesn't know you exist." (slight sadness)
lighting: golden hour glow
```

### Scene 7: Outro (1:25-1:30)

```
Close-up, woman sets mug down firmly, 
"You're not self-employed." with finality, 
"You're unemployed. With an app." (lets it land)
"And the gig is losing." (slight anger)
"Rant over." (small nod)
fade out, 
lighting: warm fade
```
