# LinkedIn Influencers (I Learned Everything From Failure)

**Episode:** 030  
**Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Status:** Ready for script

LinkedIn influencers all learned "everything" from firing someone.

"I fired him yesterday. He cried. I watched. That's when I learned everything."

You didn't learn everything. You learned one thing. Badly.

And you're not a CEO. You're a bloke with a logo.

"Thought leader." That's just "wanker" in corporate.

"Guru." Wanker with a course.

"Visionary." Wanker who talks a lot.

Every LinkedIn post is the same. Dramatic hook. "I fired him." Build tension. "He started crying." Reveal lesson. "I learned everything." Humble brag. "Now we're at $10M ARR." Call to action. "Follow for more!"

Nobody learns anything. Everyone cringes.

"Humblebrag: I'm exhausted from my third yacht viewing."

Just brag. Or don't. But stop humblebragging.

My nan thought leading was knowing where the good tea bags were. She was a thought leader. In her kitchen.

LinkedIn is Made Up CEO Simulator.

"Excited to announce I'm now VP of Delegating!"

You didn't announce anything. You typed it. Into LinkedIn.

Rant over. Unfollowing everyone. My feed is peaceful. And CEO-free.
