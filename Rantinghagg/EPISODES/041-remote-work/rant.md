# Remote Work Is Just Work From Home With Better Wifi

**Episode:** 041  
**Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Status:** Ready for script

Remote work is just work from home with better wifi.

Same work. Same hours. Just different location.

"You're at home!" = "You're available!"

"No commute" = "You can answer emails from bed."

Work-life balance used to mean 9-5.

Now it means "work when you want."

Which means "work all the time."

My nan worked 9-5. She went home. She had a life.

I work from home. I'm always home.

Which means I'm always working.

I have a home office. It's my kitchen table.

"Set up a proper workspace." = £500 desk.

"Invest in yourself." = buy furniture.

My nan's office was the kitchen.

She cooked. And worked.

Remote work: 40% of workers. 73% more productive.

65% no boundaries.

Rant over. Closing the laptop. It's 5 PM.
