# Video Script: The Digital Detox Journey™

**Episode:** 007  
**Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Format:** TikTok / Reels / Shorts  
**Voice:** RantingHagg (British, dry, amused)

---

## [0:00-0:10] OPENING

*[Hagg in armchair, holding phone, scrolling]*

**HAGG:**
So digital detoxes are a thing now.

*[looks up at camera]*

People are paying actual money. Hundreds of pounds.

To go to a retreat. Where someone takes their phone away.

*[pauses]*

Something your mum used to do for free. When you were being annoying.

---

## [0:10-0:25] THE HOOK

**HAGG:**
And the first thing they do when they arrive at the no-phone retreat?

*[mimes taking photo]*

Take a photo. For Instagram.

*"Day one of my digital detox journey! 🌿✨"*

*[deadpan]*

That's not a detox, love.

That's a holiday. With better marketing.

---

## [0:25-0:40] THE ANXIETY

**HAGG:**
Then they spend three days "reconnecting with nature."

*[air quotes]*

Which apparently means sitting in a field. Feeling anxious.

Because they don't know what's happening on Twitter.

*[leans forward]*

Is Twitter still there?

Has someone said something stupid?

*[sips tea]*

Of course someone's said something stupid.

Someone's always saying something stupid.

That's the entire business model.

---

## [0:40-0:55] THE RELAPSE

**HAGG:**
Day three they crack.

Someone finds a laptop in the office.

Checks their emails. "Just quickly."

*[shakes head]*

Just quickly.

That's how every relapse starts.

Nobody ever checks their emails. Slowly. And deliberately.

---

## [0:55-1:10] THE PAYOFF

**HAGG:**
Then they come home.

Post a fifteen-slide carousel. About their "transformative experience."

Spend four hours. Responding to comments about it.

And the next day?

*[sips tea]*

They're doom-scrolling. At 2am. Again.

---

## [1:10-1:15] OUTRO

**HAGG:**
The detox lasted seventy-two hours.

The Instagram content about the detox?

*[sets mug down]*

Will last forever.

That's not how detoxing works, love.

*[fade out on her shaking head]*
