## 4. Brand Partnerships

### Perfect-Fit Partners (Comedic, On-Brand)

**Tea Companies** ☕
- Yorkshire Tea, PG Tips, Twinings, Clipper
- "The Hagg's official fuel. The only thing that hasn't disappointed me."
- Sponsored rants where she sips the product naturally
- Co-branded limited edition tea tin with Hagg character and quotes

**Glasses/Opticians** 👓
- Specsavers ("Should've gone to Specsavers" is already a meme)
- Warby Parker, Bailey Nelson
- "These glasses help me see the absurdity in higher definition. Wish they didn't."

**Sensible Shoe Companies** 👟
- Clarks, Hotter, Skechers comfort range
- "Comfortable shoes for people who have better things to do than suffer."
- Comedy angle: reviewing ridiculous shoes vs. sensible ones

**Book Publishers** 📚
- Waterstones, WHSmith partnerships for ebook/physical book
- "Books the Hagg recommends" — curated list

**Greeting Card Companies** 💌
- Moonpig, Scribbler, Paperchase
- RantingHagg greeting card range (see below)

**Stationery Brands** 📝
- Notebooks, pens, planners with Hagg quotes
- "A notebook for writing down everything that's wrong with everyone."

### Partnership Rules
1. Product must be something the Hagg would genuinely use or approve of
2. Ad reads must be in character — never break the fourth wall
3. No "use code" style calls to action — weave naturally into content
4. Maximum 2 sponsored posts per month to maintain trust
5. All partnerships disclosed transparently (but comedically: "They're paying me to say this but I'd have said it anyway")
