# RantingHagg ☕

*Sharp-witted observational comedy about the absurdity of modern life*

---

Right, so... you've found the official home of **RantingHagg** — your nan if your nan had a philosophy degree and a Twitter account.

I'm not angry. I'm not bitter. I'm *bemused*. I've lived long enough to know better, and I watch the modern world with a cup of tea I never actually drink, peering over reading glasses I don't technically need.

I love humanity. I really do. I just can't *believe* you lot most of the time.
